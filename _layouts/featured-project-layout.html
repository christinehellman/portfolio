SBT Partners

SBT Partners is a “Technology as a Service” company, that provides cloud services and solutions in the way you use and pay for your utilities. You bundle the services you need, and only pay for what you use. They also provide consulting services to increase IT efficiency and help companies plan for the future.

DESIGN

The first thing that I learned was that Hikashop’s demo site of their product uses a custom theme that is an additional purchase. Everything I knew by inspecting the styles no longer had any reign on what I had to work on.

DEVELOPMENT

I loved working on the development of this site, because it was so different from the image heavy sites I normally work on. I enjoyed brining backgrounds to life with a little css, which I found to be very delightful. I feel like on this site I was really able to creatively express the ideas of this company through a modern design.
