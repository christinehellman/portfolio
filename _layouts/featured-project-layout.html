SBT Partners

Create a modern, clutter-free website that promotes the technology solutions they offer, as well as featuring their explanatory videos.

GOALS

  • Reduce, and rewrite the copy describing what they do
  • Feature their videos on as many pages as possible
  • Increase whitespace, and brighten the feel of their website

RESPONSIBILITIES

Planning, UI Design, frontend development

SEE IT IN ACTION

SBT Partners

SBT Partners is a “Technology as a Service” company, that provides cloud services and solutions in the way you use and pay for your utilities. You bundle the services you need, and only pay for what you use. They also provide consulting services to increase IT efficiency and help companies plan for the future.

view site

DESIGN

I wanted to keep the colors clean, fresh, and provide a simple understanding of the services the company offers at a glance. I stuck with blue, a color that always seems associated with tech, but decided to brighten the intensity with gradients and overlays.
